The History of our Hotel

The Green Towers Hotel is in Pasadena, California. The hotel was built in 1893 , and was later expanded with two additional buildings in 1898 and 1903, creating a complex of three structures. The Green Towers Hotel is the home of the Valley Hunt Club and the Tournament of Roses association.
